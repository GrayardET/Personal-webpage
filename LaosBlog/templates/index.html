    This is about me

    Part One

    According to the two CaUC Color Test I did, I’ve gotten the result of “green”, which indicate a person who value competency and want to understand and control the realities of life. “Green” is highly associated with the ability of analyzing, solving problems, and developing systems. The test result also indicates that I’m a critical thinker, who don’t express my emotions openly and discuss my personal issues oftenly, but do experience deep feelings. The world of ideas and reasons is important to me, which also implies that I love intelligence, logic, and dislike redundancy.
    Personalities like observant and understanding will allow me to observe the market demands and create useful programs. Critical thinking will be beneficial for me to carefully pick mistakes in the codes and try to improve the function of my apps as much as possible.

    Strength

    "Knowledgeable, Patience, Creativity, Determination, Enthusiasm, Flexibility, Curiosity"

    My creativity plays an important part in my daily life. I never did identical job like others who copy ideas off each other. Whenever there’s a project assigned, I will always complete it in a creative way to reach my own standard. Therefore it allows me to fill my apps and games with original ideas and to be more outstanding. On the other hand, enthusiasm is considered to be the most important factor that makes computing a lifelong career, because it is boring to the majority in terms of sitting in front of the computer and writing codes.
